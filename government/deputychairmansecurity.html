About


Ben Gaunt, Deputy Chairman of State Security of the Free Union, is a loyal, tough, and intellegent political officer. He leads the Committee for State Security with an iron fist, and works valiantly to brings terrorists and anti-unionists to justice.

Ben Gaunt, born in 2007, was a high ranking federal police officer under the United States Government. Although he certainly did not agree with their capitalistic ideals, he needed to get money somehow to feed his family. But, when the people's army requested assistance from him, he decided to use the US police forces to help the revolution before his superiors even knew what happened.

After the revolution, Chairman of State Security Lara Santas, as reward for his commendabe efforts, appointed him as his deputy. Without Ben Gaunt, the Committee for State Security could not possibly be as efficient and rigid as it is today.
